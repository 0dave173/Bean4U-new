Bicerin

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • Hot chocolate (thick, about 60–80 ml)
  • Whole milk or cream (optional layer)

Preparation

  1. In a small glass, layer hot chocolate, then espresso, then a touch of milk or cream.
  2. Serve without stirring to appreciate the layers; stir to combine when drinking.
