Vienna

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate shavings

Preparation

  1. Brew espresso into cup
  2. Top with thick layer of whipped cream
  3. Sprinkle with chocolate shavings
  4. Serve with glass of water
