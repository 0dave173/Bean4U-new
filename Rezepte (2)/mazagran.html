Mazagran

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp fresh lemon zest
  • Ice cube (large)

Preparation

  1. Blend espresso, sugar, and lemon zest until sugar dissolves.
  2. Pour into a glass and add the large ice cube.
  3. Stir once, taste, and add a splash of cold water if needed.
  4. Enjoy the sharp, citrusy finish.
