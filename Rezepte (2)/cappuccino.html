Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 100ml steamed milk
  • 100ml milk foam

Preparation

  1. Brew espresso into a preheated cup
  2. Steam milk to create thick foam (1:1 milk to foam)
  3. Pour milk at an angle to mix with espresso
  4. Spoon foam on top to create distinct layers
  5. Optional: Dust with cocoa powder
