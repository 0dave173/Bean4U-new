Coffee Milk

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso
  • 2 cups milk (whole, almond, oat, etc.)
  • 1–2 Tbsp honey or flavored syrup

Preparation

  1. Warm milk gently (do not boil).
  2. Blend espresso into the milk.
  3. Sweeten with honey or syrup.
  4. Whisk until frothy, then pour into a mug.
  5. Top with a dusting of cocoa or cinnamon if desired.
