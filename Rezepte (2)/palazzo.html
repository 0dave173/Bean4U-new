Palazzo

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • Steamed milk (about 60 ml)
  • Thin layer of milk foam

Preparation

  1. Pull espresso into a cup, add steamed milk and a thin cap of foam.
  2. Serve immediately—similar to a small latte.
