Percolator Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coarse coffee grounds
  • 4 cups water
  • Metal percolator

Preparation

  1. Place grounds in percolator basket.
  2. Fill with water up to the top‑ring.
  3. Heat on stove or induction until water starts to bubble.
  4. Let it percolate multiple cycles (cycle ≈ 30 s).
  5. When color darkens to a deep brown, remove from heat.
  6. Serve immediately; add milk or sugar if desired.
