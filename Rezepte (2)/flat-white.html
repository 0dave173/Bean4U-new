Flat White

Ingredients

  • 2 shots espresso (60ml)
  • 150ml microfoam milk

Preparation

  1. Brew double espresso into a 180ml cup
  2. Steam milk to create velvety microfoam
  3. Pour milk gently from low height
  4. Create a thin, flat layer of foam (0.5cm)
