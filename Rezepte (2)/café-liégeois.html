Café Liégeois

Ingredients

  • Strong coffee (cooled)
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings

Preparation

  1. Place 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream in a tall glass.
  2. Pour cooled strong coffee over the ice cream.
  3. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings; serve with a spoon.
