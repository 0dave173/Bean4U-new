Icede Coffee

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso (or 2 tsp brewed coffee)
  • ¾ cup cold milk or cream
  • 1–2 Tbsp sugar (or syrup)
  • Ice cubes

Preparation

  1. Brew and cool the espresso.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the espresso over the ice.
  4. Add cold milk/cream and sugar.
  5. Stir well and serve.
