Café Zorro

Ingredients

  • 2 shots espresso
  • Hot water (about 60–80 ml)

Preparation

  1. Pull two espresso shots into a cup.
  2. Add hot water to dilute to the strength of an Americano but with a bolder flavor.
  3. Serve straight—often enjoyed without milk.
