Espresso Romano

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1 thin slice of lemon (peel)

Preparation

  1. Serve the espresso with a thin lemon slice on the side.
  2. Rub the lemon peel on the rim or squeeze a tiny amount over the espresso to taste.
  3. Enjoy immediately.
