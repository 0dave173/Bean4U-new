Cold Brew

Ingredients

  • 100g coarse ground coffee
  • 1 liter cold water

Preparation

  1. Combine coffee and water in jar
  2. Stir gently to saturate grounds
  3. Steep for 12-24 hours in fridge
  4. Filter through cheesecloth or paper
  5. Serve over ice (dilute 1:1 with water)
