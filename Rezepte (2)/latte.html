Latte

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 200ml steamed milk
  • 1cm milk foam

Preparation

  1. Brew espresso into a 200ml cup
  2. Steam milk to 60-65°C with microfoam
  3. Pour milk into espresso from low height
  4. Finish with a small foam layer
  5. short Explaination
