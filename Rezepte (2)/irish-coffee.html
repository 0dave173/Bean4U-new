a Irish Coffee Recipe

Irish Coffee

18+

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 40ml Irish whiskey
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 50ml lightly whipped cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat glass with hot water
  2. Add sugar and coffee, stir to dissolve
  3. Add whiskey and stir gently
  4. Float cold cream over back of spoon
  5. Do not stir after adding cream
