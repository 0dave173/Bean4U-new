Nitro Coffee

Ingredients

  • 200ml cold brew concentrate
  • Nitrogen gas

Preparation

  1. Prepare cold brew concentrate
  2. Charge in keg with nitrogen at 30-40psi
  3. Chill to 2-4°C for 24 hours
  4. Serve through nitro tap
  5. Pour at 45° angle to create cascade
