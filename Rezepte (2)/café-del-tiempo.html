Café del Tiempo

Ingredients

  • 1 espresso shot
  • Equal volume of hot water (or hot milk to taste)
  • Optional: lemon peel or sugar

Preparation

  1. Prepare espresso and dilute with hot water to create a balanced, mellow drink.
  2. Adjust with milk or sweetener if desired; traditionally a comforting combination.
  3. Serve hot.
