Drip Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coffee grounds (medium‑fine)
  • 4 cups hot water (about 93 °C)
  • Metal or paper filter

Preparation

  1. Set filter in brewer and pre‑warm with hot water.
  2. Place grounds in filter.
  3. Slowly pour hot water over grounds, giving the filter every 30 s.
  4. Let water dribble through to the carafe.
  5. Serve hot; add milk or sweetener if desired.
