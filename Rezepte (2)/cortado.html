Cortado

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 30ml warm milk

Preparation

  1. Brew espresso into small glass
  2. Heat milk to 60°C (no foam)
  3. Pour equal parts milk to espresso
  4. Serve in 60-80ml glass
