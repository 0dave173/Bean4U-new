Doppio

Ingredients

  • 18-20g coffee grounds
  • 60ml water

Preparation

  1. Grind coffee fine like espresso
  2. Tamp with 30lbs pressure
  3. Extract for 25-30 seconds
  4. Serve in preheated demitasse cup
