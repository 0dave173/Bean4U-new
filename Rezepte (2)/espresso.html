Americano

Ingredients

  • 18-20g finely ground coffee beans
  • 30ml water

Preparation

  1. Grind coffee beans to a fine consistency
  2. Tamp the grounds evenly in the portafilter
  3. Brew for 25-30 seconds at 9 bar pressure
  4. Serve immediately in a preheated cup
