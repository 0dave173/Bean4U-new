Lungo

Ingredients

  • 18g coffee grounds
  • 50-60ml water

Preparation

  1. Grind coffee slightly coarser than espresso
  2. Tamp normally
  3. Extract for 45-60 seconds
  4. Stop extraction when stream lightens
