Macchiato

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 1-2 tsp steamed milk foam

Preparation

  1. Brew espresso into a small cup
  2. Steam a small amount of milk
  3. "Stain" the espresso with a spoon of foam
  4. Serve immediately
