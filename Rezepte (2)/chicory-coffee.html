Chicory Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed black coffee
  • ¼ cup chicory root (roasted & ground)
  • 1–2 Tbsp sugar (optional)

Preparation

  1. Use a French press or drip basket with chicory coarsely ground.
  2. Brew at 90–95 °C for 4–5 min.
  3. Strain normally, sweeten if needed.
  4. Enjoy the nutty, slightly smoky flavor.
