Café Corretto

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1 tsp grappa, sambuca, or brandy (choose your favorite)

Preparation

  1. Pour the spirit into the espresso cup and add the hot espresso over it.
  2. Stir gently and serve hot.
