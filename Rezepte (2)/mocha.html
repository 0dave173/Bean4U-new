Mocha

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30ml)
  • 200ml steamed milk
  • 20g chocolate chips
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Preparation

  1. Add chocolate chips to serving cup
  2. Brew espresso over the chocolate
  3. Stir to combine
  4. Add steamed milk and gentle foam
  5. Top with whipped cream if desired
