Café Cubano

Ingredients

  • 2–3 tbsp finely ground dark roast coffee
  • Cold water for espresso machine or stovetop
  • 2 tsp sugar (per 1–2 shots) — adjust to taste

Preparation

  1. Brew a small strong pot of espresso or moka (2 shots).
  2. In a cup, mix the first small splash of espresso with sugar and whisk until foamy (espuma).
  3. Pour the rest of the hot espresso over the foam, stir gently and serve.
