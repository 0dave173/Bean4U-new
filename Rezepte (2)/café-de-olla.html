Café de Olla

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp coarsely ground coffee
  • 1/4 cup piloncillo or brown sugar (adjust)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Optional: star anise, orange peel

Preparation

  1. Bring water, piloncillo, and cinnamon to a simmer in a pot.
  2. Add coffee grounds, reduce heat and simmer 3–5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, let settle 2 minutes, strain into mugs and serve.
