Café Bombón

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso (30 ml)
  • Sweetened condensed milk (about 30-40 ml)

Preparation

  1. Pour condensed milk into the bottom of a small glass.
  2. Slowly pour a freshly brewed espresso shot over the milk so layers form.
  3. Stir before drinking or serve layered for effect.
