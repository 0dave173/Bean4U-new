Marocchino

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • Steamed milk foam (small)

Preparation

  1. Dust cocoa in the bottom of a small glass.
  2. Pour espresso over cocoa and top with a small layer of milk foam.
  3. Dust extra cocoa and serve.
