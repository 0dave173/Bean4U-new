Café au Lait

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso (or 2 shots)
  • ¼ cup boiling water
  • ¼ cup steamed milk

Preparation

  1. Pour espresso into a preheated cup.
  2. Add boiling water to reach half‑cup total.
  3. Steam milk to 65–70 °C.
  4. Gently pour milk over the espresso + water.
  5. Stir lightly, then serve hot.
