Café Brûlot

Ingredients

  • Strong brewed coffee or espresso (enough for serving)
  • 2–3 tbsp brandy or dark rum
  • Peel of 1 orange and 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Spices: cinnamon stick, cloves

Preparation

  1. Combine spirits, citrus peels, sugar and spices in a pot and warm gently.
  2. Add coffee and light carefully to flambé for a few seconds (optional, be safe).
  3. Extinguish flame, strain into cups and serve hot.
