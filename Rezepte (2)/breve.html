Breve

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso
  • ¼ cup steamed half‑cream (50/50 milk‑cream)
  • ¼ cup espresso (extra shot)

Preparation

  1. Steam half‑cream until silky.
  2. Combine equal parts espresso and steamed half‑cream.
  3. Serve immediately in a latte glass.
  4. Optional: sprinkle a pinch of nutmeg.
