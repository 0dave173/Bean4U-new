Cà Phê Trứng (Egg Coffee)

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 shot strong coffee or 60 ml brewed coffee

Preparation

  1. Whisk egg yolk with condensed milk until frothy and creamy.
  2. Pour hot coffee into a cup, top with the egg cream.
  3. Drink by spooning the creamy layer into coffee — serve warm.
