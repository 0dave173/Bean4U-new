Glace

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee (cold or room‑temp)
  • ¼–½ cup ice
  • 1 Tbsp sugar or simple syrup
  • Milk or cream to taste

Preparation

  1. Place brewed coffee, ice, and sweetener in a shaker or blender.
  2. Shake/blend until frothy and cold.
  3. Pour into a glass, top with a splash of milk or cream.
  4. Serve immediately for a refreshing iced coffee.
