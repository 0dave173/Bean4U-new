Café Shakerato

Ingredients

  • 2 shots freshly brewed espresso (chilled briefly)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: simple syrup

Preparation

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice, add espresso (and syrup if using).
  2. Shake vigorously 20–30 seconds until frothy and chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled glass and serve.
