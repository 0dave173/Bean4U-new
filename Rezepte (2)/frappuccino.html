Frappuccino

Ingredients

  • 30 ml espresso shot
  • ¾ cup milk (whole or plant‑based)
  • 1 cup ice (crushed)
  • 1–2 Tbsp flavored syrup (vanilla, mocha, etc.)

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a tall glass, drizzle syrup on top.
  4. Optional: add whipped cream and dust with cocoa.
  5. Watch a quick Frappuccino blend YouTube guide.
