Ristretto

Ingredients

  • 18-20g finely ground coffee
  • 15-20ml water

Preparation

  1. Grind coffee slightly finer than espresso
  2. Tamp firmly (30lbs pressure)
  3. Brew for 15-20 seconds only
  4. Stop at first signs of blonding
