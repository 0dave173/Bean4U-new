Café Touba

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp coarse dark roast coffee
  • 1–2 tsp grains of selim (guinea pepper) or substitution: black pepper + clove
  • Sugar to taste

Preparation

  1. Grind or lightly crush the pepper/clove mixture and roast briefly with coffee.
  2. Boil water and add coffee + spice mix; simmer for several minutes.
  3. Strain and sweeten to taste. Serve hot.
