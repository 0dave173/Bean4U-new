Pharisäer Kaffee

Ingredients

  • Freshly brewed strong coffee
  • 2 tbsp dark rum (or to taste)
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Whipped cream to top

Preparation

  1. Pour sugar and rum into a heatproof glass.
  2. Add hot coffee, stir to dissolve sugar and mix with rum.
  3. Top generously with whipped cream; do not stir—the cream insulates aroma.
